About Me

The major I am currently taking is Digital Art and Design. I love this field because it allows you to be expressive in numerous ways; there is no right or wrong answer. Graphic design is especially exciting because one uses all of the elements such as contents, images, typography, and illustrations as a creative language to express a thought or an idea, personal views or experiences, or anything they want to share with the world. After graduation, I will probably go back to being an eyelash technician but my goal is to try to find work within this field.
